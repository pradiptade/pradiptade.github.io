MoSyS Lab Mission

The mission of the Mobile Systems and Solutions, MoSyS, lab is to explore novel techniques to enrich the mobile computing space. Our goal is to leverage insights and technologies from different domains of computer systems, like cloud computing, computer vision, machine learning, and apply them to solve research challenges in mobile computing space.
